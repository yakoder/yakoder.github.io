Lies Companies Tell

03 Sep 2025 18:47
Jon Warren

When unsubscribing from newsletters: "It can take up to 10 days to process changes." - This is technically a lie. Orders don't take "10 days" to process, they are processed immediately. Newsletter (new) subscriptions don't take "10 days" to add them to the list. So it it technically possible for removals to be processed immediately. The only reason -- the only reason -- companies purposefully delay removal from mailing lists is so that they can force unwanted SPAM on people who have made it clear they do not want SPAM.
